AI PING PONG GAME







Creator:Bhavya Sahu

Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight and 3-4 feet away from you.
  2. Move your right wrist, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  3. Now press the PLAY button, it's time to play!
  4. The red paddel is your paddel and it will move according to your movement of the right wrist.
